Sanrio

Sanrio is a Japanese company famous for creating beloved characters like Hello Kitty, My Melody, and Kuromi. Founded in 1960 by Shintaro Tsuji, Sanrio promotes the "small gift, big smile" philosophy. Their characters, known for their cute designs and positive messages, appear on a wide range of merchandise and are adored worldwide.

Kuromi


Personality - mischievous and spooky
"I may look tough, but my heart is sweet." — Kuromi

Kuromi is a popular character from Sanrio, the same company behind Hello Kitty. She is known for her mischievous and punky personality, often depicted wearing a black jester's hat with a pink skull on the front. Despite her tough exterior, Kuromi has a softer side, enjoying activities like writing in her diary and having a fondness for cute things. Her unique blend of edginess and charm has made her a favorite among fans who appreciate her rebellious yet endearing nature.

Cinnamoroll


Personality - gentle and sweet nature
"Believe in yourself and anything is possible!" — Cinnamoroll

Cinnamoroll is a charming character created by Sanrio, known for his fluffy white fur, long ears, and signature blue eyes. Originally introduced in 2001, Cinnamoroll quickly became a beloved symbol of innocence and friendship. His story revolves around his adventures with friends in Café Cinnamon, where he was discovered by the café owner. With his sweet and gentle demeanor, Cinnamoroll continues to captivate fans worldwide through his cute and endearing appearances on various Sanrio merchandise and media.
